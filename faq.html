Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our handcrafted collections, export process, and partnership opportunities.

❖ What products do you offer?

Venus Handicrafts offers a wide range of decorative handicrafts including:

  • Iron Handicrafts – candle holders, wall art, garden stakes
  • Glass Handicrafts – handblown vases, decorative bowls
  • Brass Handicrafts – idols, trays, lamps, and diyas
  • Wood Handicrafts – serving boards, carved boxes, wall panels
  • Aluminium Handicrafts – hammered trays, vases, tea light holders
  • Home Décor – mirrors, showpieces, wall hangings
  • Tableware – ceramic & wooden dinner sets, platters
  • Candle Accessories – lanterns, holders, and aroma décor

We also offer custom designs based on buyer specifications.

❖ Do you export internationally?

Yes, absolutely! Venus Handicrafts has been exporting globally since 1972. We currently ship to over 45+ countries including the USA, Canada, United Kingdom, Germany, France, Australia, UAE, and many more. Our team handles all export documentation, customs clearance, and logistics to ensure smooth delivery worldwide.

We are a recognized export house with EPCH membership and comply with international quality standards.

❖ What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our standard MOQ for wholesale orders is 100 pieces per design for most product categories. However, we offer flexibility:

  • Sample orders: As low as 1–5 pieces for quality evaluation.
  • Trial orders: 50 pieces per SKU for new buyers.
  • Custom designs: MOQ may vary depending on design complexity and raw material sourcing.

Contact our export team to discuss your specific requirements — we are happy to accommodate genuine inquiries.

❖ How can I place an order?

Placing an order with Venus Handicrafts is simple:

  1. Browse our product catalog or share your design reference.
  2. Request a quote with pricing, MOQ, and lead time.
  3. Approve samples (we provide pre-production samples).
  4. Confirm your purchase order with 30-50% advance payment.
  5. Production begins — quality checks at each stage.
  6. Balance payment before shipment, followed by logistics coordination.

You can reach us via the Contact page or email us directly at trade@venushandicrafts.com to start your order.

❖ What materials are used in your handicrafts?

We use premium, sustainable, and ethically sourced materials:

  • Iron: High-grade wrought iron with rust-resistant powder coating.
  • Glass: Lead-free, eco-friendly handblown glass.
  • Brass: Solid brass with anti-tarnish lacquer or natural patina.
  • Wood: Mango wood, sheesham (Indian rosewood), and reclaimed timber — all from certified sources.
  • Aluminium: Recyclable aluminium with hand-hammered finishes.
  • Ceramic & Terracotta: Lead-free glazes, food-safe for tableware.

All finishes are non-toxic and compliant with international safety standards (REACH, Prop 65 upon request).

❖ What is the typical production lead time?

Lead times depend on order volume and design complexity:

  • Sample orders: 10–15 working days
  • Small orders (under 500 pcs): 25–30 days
  • Bulk orders (1000+ pcs): 45–60 days
  • Custom designs: Additional 2–3 weeks for tooling and prototyping

We provide regular production updates and can accommodate urgent orders with expedited timelines (additional charges may apply).

❖ Do you offer custom design & OEM manufacturing?

Yes! We specialize in custom manufacturing and OEM/ODM projects. Share your sketches, reference images, or CAD files, and our in-house design team will develop prototypes. We’ve partnered with global brands for exclusive collections. Minimum quantities for custom designs start at 300–500 pieces depending on complexity.

We also offer private labeling and custom packaging solutions.

❖ What are your payment terms?

We offer flexible and secure payment options for international buyers:

  • Advance payment: 30% deposit, 70% balance against shipping documents (TT/Bank Transfer).
  • Letter of Credit (L/C): At sight or usance L/C from reputable banks.
  • For existing clients: Negotiated terms available based on order history.

We accept payments in USD, EUR, GBP, and INR. All transactions are fully compliant with international trade regulations.
