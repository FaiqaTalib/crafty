For over five decades, Venus Handicrafts has stood as a beacon of authentic Indian craftsmanship, exporting exceptional decorative pieces that tell stories of heritage, passion, and precision.

Rooted in Heritage, Rising with Craftsmanship

Founded in 1972 by visionary artisan Shri Radheyshyam Agarwal, Venus Handicrafts began as a small workshop in the heart of Jaipur's craft quarter. With a deep respect for age-old techniques — from hand-carved wood to intricate brass inlay — the company soon gained recognition for its unwavering quality and artistic integrity.

What started as a local endeavor evolved into an international success story. By the 1990s, Venus Handicrafts became a trusted partner for renowned home décor brands across Europe and North America. Today, the second generation continues the legacy, blending traditional artistry with modern design sensibilities, while empowering over 500+ skilled artisans across Rajasthan.

Our journey reflects a seamless blend of timeless tradition and global vision, making Venus Handicrafts a name synonymous with excellence in the world of decorative handicrafts.
